About Me

A passionate and driven Electronics and Communication Engineering undergraduate at VIT Vellore, with a strong focus on VLSI system design, digital hardware architectures, and ASIC implementation. My expertise lies in RTL design, approximate and mixed-precision arithmetic, and hardware acceleration for signal processing and AI workloads, supported by hands-on experience in FPGA prototyping and full ASIC flows up to GDS-II. I am deeply motivated by research and innovation in energy-efficient computing and silicon-level optimization, and I constantly strive to translate complex algorithms into high-performance, real-world hardware solutions.
